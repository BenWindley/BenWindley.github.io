About Me

I do pixel art when I'm not coding, participating in game jams, or working on projects. A majority of my projects contain my pixel art, however a minority of my art am I actually satisfied with.

I used to study maths before changing to to a life of programming and game design. I believe that programming and game design require an equally diverse set of skills that stem off logic, inspiration, and experience, areas of which I am constantly exploring.

If you have any questions about my projects or wish to see/play them then feel free to ask!
